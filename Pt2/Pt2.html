What are Personal Values?

Personal values are what matter most to us, the qualities and behaviors that drive us and influence our actions. Personal values are a component of the moral standard that governs our behavior and determines who we really are. They are the qualities we value, the ones that are crucial to our health but also satisfaction in life. The simplest method to define personal values is to consider your character as well as habits.
