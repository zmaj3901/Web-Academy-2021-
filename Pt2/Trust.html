"Trustworthy"

10th Grade

By Zainab Majidi

3/18/22

"What does it mean to be Trustworthy?" "How do I live by this Personal Value?"
  • To be Trustworthy means to have people rely on you and confide with their secrets or something important knowing that it won't end up around the whole town the next day.
  • I live by this personal value by trying to always make sure that people can talk to me if they have concerns and I try not to engage in gossip which will therefore, allow me to better retain secrets and be a trustworthy person.
